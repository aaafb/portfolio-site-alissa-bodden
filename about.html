About Me

Hey there! I'm a Product Designer with over 3 years of taking ideas from rough sketches to polished features people actually love to use. I create experiences that marry function and visual delight.

During my time at Crossrope as their UI/UX Designer, I got to work on all sorts of features that improved usability, engagement, and conversion rates. My process thrives on collaboration, empathy, and communication: working closely with developers and stakeholders to balance business goals, user needs, and technical constraints.
